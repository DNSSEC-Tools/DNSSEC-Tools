Are you using DNSSEC?

This site tests whether your browser is being protected by a DNSSEC Validating Resolver. Depending on your browser and/or operating environment, you'll see either a thumbs up or thumbs down image above. In order for the thumbs up image to appear, all your DNS requests need to be validating.
